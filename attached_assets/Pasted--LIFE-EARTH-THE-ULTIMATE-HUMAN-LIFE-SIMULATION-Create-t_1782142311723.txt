# LIFE EARTH – THE ULTIMATE HUMAN LIFE SIMULATION

Create the most realistic multiplayer life simulation ever built.

Players do not create adult characters. Every player enters the world as a newborn baby delivered by their mother and begins life from birth.

The world never resets. It runs continuously 24/7 as a living universe.

## Birth System

* Every player starts as a newborn baby.
* Each player is born into a randomly generated family.
* Parents have jobs, wealth, education, reputation, health, and personalities.
* Some players are born rich.
* Some are born poor.
* Some are born into loving families.
* Some are born into difficult environments.
* Birth location can be anywhere on Earth.

## Real Human Growth

Characters must grow naturally.

Stages:

* Newborn
* Infant
* Toddler
* Child
* Teenager
* Young Adult
* Adult
* Elderly

Players cannot instantly become adults.

Every year of life matters.

Childhood experiences shape future outcomes.

There is no final level.
